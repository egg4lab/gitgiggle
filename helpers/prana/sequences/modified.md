# Modified Sequences

## Shoulder injury (impingement, rotator cuff)
- Knees-down chaturanga always
- Skip jump-back/jump-through; step instead
- Hold downward dog briefly between sides; no full vinyasa
- Modify or skip: Purvattanasana, Bhujapidasana, Kurmasana arm wraps,
  Urdhva Dhanurasana
- Finishing: skip Sirsasana variations using arms; use rope/wall if needed

## Wrist issue
- Fists or forearms instead of palms in plank/chaturanga
- Skip arm balances entirely until cleared
- Use blocks under hands for forward folds requiring weight bearing

## Lower back / SI joint
- Skip Supta Konasana, Setu Bandhasana
- Modified Marichyasana (no deep twist)
- No dropbacks; gentle Urdhva Dhanurasana with feet wider
- Extra attention to bandha cues

## Knee issue
- Skip half-lotus variants (Ardha Baddha sequences)
- Cross-legged or Sukhasana for finishing lotus poses
- Modified Triang Mukha — sit on block, no full fold
